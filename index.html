About

I am a PhD student in Informatics at the University of Edinburgh (ICSA, School of Informatics), working on networks and systems for AI. My research spans defect-aware network-on-chip (NoC) architectures, network simulation for distributed AI workloads, and interconnect design and system software for wafer-scale AI accelerators. Before my PhD, I spent several years in the semiconductor industry at Cadence Design Systems and TSMC, working on advanced process nodes, EDA tooling, and computational lithography.
